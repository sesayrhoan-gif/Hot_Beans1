Eligibility Criteria

At Hot Beans Company, we aim to support new and aspiring web developers. To join our training and internship programmes, candidates must meet a few basic requirements.

Minimum Requirements

  • Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and web development
  • Strong interest in technology and coding
  • Ability to work in a team environment
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills

Preferred Skills

  • Knowledge of JavaScript (basic level)
  • Experience with school or personal coding projects
  • Understanding of responsive design
  • Willingness to learn and improve

Who Can Apply?

We welcome students, beginners, and anyone passionate about starting a career in web development. No professional experience is required — just motivation and commitment to learn.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help you grow into a confident developer through hands-on projects, mentorship, and real-world experience.
